## 🎯 Common Tasks

### Add a New Property
1. Click **Properties** in sidebar
2. Click **"+ Add Property"** button
3. Fill in the form
4. Click **"Save Property"**

### Add an Advertisement
1. Click **Advertisements** in sidebar
2. Click **"+ Add Advertisement"** button
3. Enter title and image URL
4. Click **"Save Advertisement"**

### Publish News Article
1. Click **News** in sidebar
2. Click **"+ Add News Article"** button
3. Fill in article details
4. Click **"Save Article"**

### Manage Users
1. Click **Users** in sidebar
2. Use search to find specific users
3. Click **Edit** or **Delete** on user cards
